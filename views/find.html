Find

Find Alternate Reality Games by theme and whether it is ongoing or concluded

Possible searches:

  1. List themes: /find/theme
  2. All ongoing: /find/ongoing
  3. All ended: /find/concluded
  4. By theme: /find/{theme}(accepts ID or name)
  5. Ongoing by theme: /find/ongoing/{theme}
  6. Concluded by theme: /find/concluded/{theme}
